Grey Wolf

The grey wolf is generally monogamous, with mated pairs usually remaining together for life.

  1. Habitat: Tundra, Mountain areas, Woodlands, Forests, Grasslands, and Deserts.
  2. Lifespan: 5-6 years
  3. Diet: Solely meat!
  4. Size: 120 - 180lbs, 2.6 - 2.8ft height, 3.4 - 5.2ft length.

They're viscious but live in packs and are extremely adorable but you need to stay away cause they'll eat ya.
